CS Tip Apr 3, 2026
The 3-sentence rule: resolve every ticket in one reply
The best CS agents resolve tickets in a single reply. The formula: (1) Acknowledge the problem and show empathy, (2) Give the solution clearly and directly, (3) Close the loop — tell them what happens next.

If you need more than 3 back-and-forth emails to resolve a ticket, the first reply wasn't clear enough. Train your agents on this — it directly improves your emails/ticket KPI.
✉️ Template: "Hi [name], I completely understand how frustrating this must be. I've [solution]. You'll receive [next step] within [timeframe]. Don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything else!"
